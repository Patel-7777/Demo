Dr. Bashir A. Ahmadi is a Neurologist, practicing in Ellisbridge, Ahmedabad and has an experience of 27 years in these fields. His main interest is in Headache diagnosis and management. He completed MBBS from N H L Municipal Medical College in 1979 and MD- Medicine from Sheth K. M. School of P.G and Research, Ahmedabad in 1982. He has teaching experience of about 21 years and left Smt. N. H. L. Municipal Medical College as Associate Professor of Medicine in Dec 2010. He is a member of American Academy of Neurology, Life Member Indian Medical Association, Life Member Association of Physicians of India, Life Member Epilepsy Society of India, Life Member Indian Stroke Association, Associate Member of Neurological Society of India and Associate Life Member of Indian Academy of Neurology.
